ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Akali, and I'm an illustrator who believes in the magic of a well-placed line and the power of a vibrant color palette. My artistic journey began with a childhood spent doodling in the margins of textbooks and dreaming up fantastical creatures. That playful spirit still guides my hand today! Growing up in the vibrant chaos of a place like Bahamas, with its lively streets and beautiful beaches, my imagination always had plenty of fuel. I'd spend hours observing the world around me, from the intricate patterns on a seashell to the dynamic energy of people enjoying the sun. These observations, combined with countless hours lost in fantasy novels and animated films, sparked a desire to create my own visual narratives. It wasn't just about drawing; it was about building entire worlds with a pencil and paper, populating them with characters that felt real to me. I specialize in creating whimsical and enchanting illustrations that aim to bring a smile to your face. Whether it's a quirky animal, a charming character, or a fantastical scene, I love infusing my work with a sense of joy and wonder. My process often involves lots of tea and a good playlist, and I'm always excited to tackle new creative challenges. I'm constantly experimenting with new digital brushes, exploring different color harmonies, and pushing the boundaries of my style. When I'm not illustrating, you'll probably find me exploring new music, sketching in my local park, or brainstorming ideas for my next big project. Let's make something beautiful and fun together!
